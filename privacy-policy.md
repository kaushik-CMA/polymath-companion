Privacy Policy – Polymath Companion

Polymath Companion respects your privacy.

Data Collection

Polymath Companion does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Local Data Storage

All data created within the app — including topics, intervals, notes, and settings — is stored locally on the user’s device only.
This data never leaves the device and is not accessible to the developer.

Internet Usage

The app does not require an internet connection for normal use.
All core features work fully offline.

Third-Party Services

Polymath Companion does not use third-party analytics, tracking tools, or external data services.

Advertisements

The current version of the app does not display advertisements.
If advertisements or monetization features are added in future versions, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

User Accounts

Polymath Companion does not require user registration or login.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future if app functionality changes.
Any updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer via the app’s official repository.
